    What is the ?

    How can I earn it?
    1. Login with your Steam account.
    2. Add your Trade URL.
    3. Add money to your account with G2A Pay or click on "Redeem g2a code" and enter the link that you have received (You will be automatically redirected to the correct case).
    4. Choose a case which you want to buy.
    5. Click on the case and then click "Open Case".
    6. Our algorithm will randomly pick an item for you and you will see a notification overlay which will show you the item you have won.
    Where can I find my Trade URL?
    1. Visit https://steamcommunity.com/id/me/tradeoffers/privacy#trade_offer_access_url
    2. Enter your Steam login and password.
    3. At the sub-item "Third-Party Sites" you will find your Trade URL.

    Please note:

    Whenever you create a new Trade URL, your old one will be rendered useless.

    Please don't forget to update it on our site when changing it.

    How will I receive my items?

    You will receive your items as a trade offer, which you can find here: https://steamcommunity.com/id/me/tradeoffers

    You just have to accept the trade request from our bot.

    Please note:

    Due to Steam restrictions, you will have to set your inventory visibility to "public" before we can send you a trade offer.

    How long does it take until I can get my items?

    It takes about 1 hour until we send you your items.

    You can visit your inventory to check, when your items will be send.

    Usually it happens instantly.

    If you don't request or accept a trade offer for 1 hour, the items will be deleted from your inventory and our bots.

    I didn't receive my item through the trade request, what should I do now?

    If you didn't receive your item or the trade was cancelled, then that's a problem on your end.

    Your account might be not able to trade at the moment, there are several reasons why that might be the case.

    For more information on trade restrictions, please follow this link: https://support.steampowered.com/kb_article.php?ref=1047-EDFM-2932

    You can always request a new trade offer from our bot via your inventory.

    If you are not able to solve the problem or you suspect that one of out bots is the root of the problem, please write us via online chat or e-mail.
